Type: Fairy

Region: Kalos

Category: Life Pokémon

Description: The embodiment of life. It can share eternal life and represents balance with Yveltal (destruction) and Zygarde (order). When it sleeps, it becomes a tree.

Special Ability: Fairy Aura (boosts Fairy-type moves).

Signature Move: Geomancy — a move that powers up greatly after charging for one turn.
