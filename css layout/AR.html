Arceus is the god of all pokemon

Normal (can change type with special Plates)

Region: Sinnoh

Category: Alpha Pokémon

Known as the creator of the Pokémon universe, Arceus is said to have shaped time, space, and antimatter — giving birth to Dialga, Palkia, and Giratina.

Ability: Multitype — changes its type depending on the Plate or Z-Crystal it holds.

Signature Move: Judgment (changes type with the Plate)
